Description

The Hermès Kelly Sellier is a highly classic model and was infat the model wich was carried by its namesake, Grace Kelly. Grace Kelly used the bag to hide her growing baby bump from paparazzi. Then the bag was re-names the Kelly. This is to this day one of the most iconic bags Hermès produce. The sellier model is a bag that has the stitching on the outside of the bag as opposed to the retourne which has the stitching inside. The Sellier model is a more formal model bag. This particular model is a very special bag, with the famous and highly sought after Horse Shoe Stamp (HSS) which means that this bag was designed by the consumer for themselves and features a tiny stamp of a horse shoe next to the Hermès Paris stamp. This model HSS is crafted from epsom leather and has brushed palladium hardware which is exceptionally rare. The colour combination is Vert Cypress and Gris Tourterelle. This bag is As New and comes as a full set.
