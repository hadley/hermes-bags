Description

Hermès Birkin was named after the uber talented Jane Birkin who was a very famous actress and singer in the 1980’s. This bag was created in the 35cm size initially as a practical bag that could carry all of the essentials needed for the modern day mother and working woman. Since its inception, the Birkin has taken on a multitude of forms, in different colours, leathers and sizes, also with may limited edition models being released. This particular bag is 2021 and is pre-loved, this bag does show signs of use, Please view images closely, the price of the bag is a reflection of the condition. This bag comes as a full set. This model is crafted from barenia fabourg leather that is in the colour Ebene which is the french word for ebony, which is the darkest of browns complemented with palladium hardware.
