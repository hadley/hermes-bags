Description

Hermès produced the Picotin bag as an hommage to the horse feed bag used by equestrians. This style of bag has two top handles that fit easily on the forearm, a strap that pulls over the bag and is secured by a lock and key, and also is usually crafted from grained leather in this instance Clemence. This Beton shade is a ultra pale grey with more tones of cream. This is a beautiful light Hermès off white shade complimented by the palladium hardware. This bag comes box fresh as a full set.
