Description

The Hermès touch is a bag that is a highly sought after bag due to the touch of exotic skin on the front flap, handles and closure straps. This hint of exotic skin gives the standard Birkin 25 a classy and chic edge without the full exotic feel. The Prun Noir and Raisin leathers are complemented perfectly with the rose gold hardware again a truly classy touch to any Hermès bag. The exotic skin on this bag is depicted by the square stamp next to the “Made in France” sign which in this case means the leather is made from Alligator Mississippensis which is destinguished by the lack of the tiny pore dot on each scale. This particular alligator skin is a Matte version which is at a higher price point from Hermès. This bag is in Excellent pre loved condition, there is one microscopic mark on the back bottom piping, and the turn lock sticker is missing. Otherwise this bag has no marks whatsoever.
