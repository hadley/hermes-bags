Description

Constance bags from Hermès are part of the quota bag selection. Quota bags fall into three models, anything Birkin model, anything Kelly model and anything Constance model. This Studio 24 is a nod to the 24 Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré where Hermès bags were first crafted. The Studio 24 Constance 18 is crafted from Veau Monsieur leather and was a limited blue box edition in 2021. The hardware is mirrored however it is inspired by a disco ball which is designed to reflect light to shine like a diamond. Please note this is a pre loved bag, there are signs of use and some of the stickers have come away. The leather shows a rub on two of the corners these are reasonably faint and the leather also shows signs of use with nail marks etc. The hardware seems to be very good showing only a few microscopic scratch marks. This bag comes with dustbag and authentication only.
